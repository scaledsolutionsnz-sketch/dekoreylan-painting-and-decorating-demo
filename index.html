About DekoReylan

A local painter who cares about the finish

DekoReylan Painting and Decorating is a Christchurch painting and decorating business built on doing the job right — the prep, the paint and the parts most people never see. The aim is simple: a finish you're proud of, done without the mess and the run-around.

Every job gets the same care, whether it's one room or the whole house, inside or out. Honest advice on colours and coatings, clear pricing up front, and a tidy site from start to finish.

  • Locally owned and operated in Christchurch
  • Tidy, dust-conscious work and daily clean-up
