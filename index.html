What we do

Painting and decorating, inside and out

From a single feature wall to a full exterior repaint, the prep is done properly so the topcoats land clean and last.

Interior painting

Walls, ceilings, doors and trim refreshed in low-odour paints — straight cut-ins and an even, roller-mark-free finish.

Exterior repaints

About DekoReylan

A Christchurch painter who treats your place like their own

DekoReylan Painting and Decorating is a small, owner-run painting business serving homeowners, landlords and property managers across Christchurch. The job runs the way you'd want it to — turn up when we say, keep the site tidy, and leave you with a finish you're happy to show off.

Whether it's freshening up one room before it goes on the market or repainting the whole exterior, you deal with the person doing the work, not a call centre.
