Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

DekoReylan Painting and Decorating respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle the information you share with us, in line with the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020.

What we collect

When you contact us or submit the quote form, we collect the details you provide — your name, phone number, email, suburb and a description of the job. That's it.

How we use it

We use your information only to respond to your enquiry, prepare a quote, and carry out and follow up on work you ask us to do. We don't sell or rent your details to anyone.

Sharing

We don't share your information with third parties except where needed to deliver your job (for example a paint supplier) or where required by law. Our online quote form is handled by a third-party form service that forwards your message to us by email.

Keeping it safe

We keep your information only as long as needed for the work and our records, and take reasonable steps to keep it secure.

Your rights

You can ask to see the information we hold about you, or ask us to correct or delete it, at any time. Call 022 190 0106.
