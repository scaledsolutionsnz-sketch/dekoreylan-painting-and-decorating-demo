Privacy Policy

How DekoReylan Painting and Decorating collects, uses and protects the information you share with us. Placeholder content for the demo.

1. Information we collect

When you contact us or request a quote through this website, we collect the details you provide — typically your name, phone number, email address, suburb and a description of the work you need. We only collect what we need to respond and quote.

2. How we use it

We use your information to reply to your enquiry, arrange a site visit, prepare a quote, carry out work, and send invoices. We may contact you about your job. We do not use your details for unrelated marketing without your consent.

3. Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We only share it where necessary to provide our services (for example, with a supplier for a specific job) or where required by law. Form submissions on this site are delivered to us by email via a third-party form service.

4. Storage & security

We take reasonable steps to keep your information secure and to retain it only as long as needed for the purpose it was collected, or as required by law.

5. Your rights

Under the Privacy Act 2020, you may request access to the personal information we hold about you and ask us to correct it. Contact us using the details below.

6. Cookies & analytics

This website may use basic cookies or analytics to understand how visitors use the site. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

For any privacy questions, email reylan@dekoreylan.co.nz or call 022 190 0106.
